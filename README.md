# F-B-Sales-Dashboard-Data-Analysis-Project
## Objective Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Food &amp; Beverage (F&amp;B) sales data to build interactive dashboards for state and city-level performance tracking. The objective was to help Regional and State Heads monitor sales, identify growth opportunities, and improve decision-making through data-driven insights.
## Project Summary
Developed an end-to-end Sales Analytics Dashboard for a Food & Beverage (F&B) business using Excel, Tableau, and Power BI.The project analyzes 2.17 lakh+ transactional records (2018–2020) to generate business insights, monitor KPIs, and support data-driven decision-making at the Regional and State level.
This project demonstrates strong skills in:
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- KPI Design & Business Metrics
- Dashboard Development
- Trend & Performance Analysis
- Business Insight Generatio

## Business Objective
To build dynamic dashboards for State & City-level Sales Review meetings, enabling:
- Store-wise performance tracking
- Manager-wise sales growth analysis
- Target vs Achievement monitoring
- Product & Meal Type performance evaluation
- Identification of revenue growth opportunities

## Key KPIs Designed
- Total Sales Revenue
- Target vs Actual Achievement %
- Store-wise Contribution
- Manager-wise Performance
- Year-over-Year Growth
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Average Bill Value
- Product Category Contribution

## Key Business Insights
- Top 3 stores contributed ~50% of total revenue
- 11 stores achieved their sales targets
- Highest sales recorded in 2020
- Significant YoY growth from 2018 to 2020
- Home Delivery & Takeaway channels show high growth potential
- Increasing breakfast variety can improve walk-in sales
